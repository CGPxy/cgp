Biography

I am currently a second year Ph.D. student in the College of Artificial Intelligence, Nankai University, supervised by Prof. Yu Dai and Prof. Jianxun Zhang. Previously, I received the Master's degree from School of Computer and Software Engineering, Xihua University in 2020, under the supervison from Prof. Zhisheng Gao.

My research interest lies in medical images analysis, especially kidney/Breast ultrasound images segmentation.
